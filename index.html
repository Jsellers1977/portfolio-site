More Detailed Story

My journey into the world of programming began when I started writing code 8 years ago. After realizing my passion for software development, I pursued my undergraduate degree in Software Development, where I initially worked with Java. However, my curiosity and fascination with web technologies led me to explore HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP.

To further enhance my knowledge and expertise, I pursued a graduate degree in Web Design & Development at Denver University. This focused program allowed me to delve deeper into the intricacies of web technologies and refine my skills specifically for web development.

Today, I am proficient in a wide range of front-end web development skills, including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and various modern frameworks such as React and Vue.js. I also have experience with back-end technologies like Node.js and Express.js.

I am passionate about staying up-to-date with the latest trends and best practices in web development, and I am always eager to take on new challenges and projects. With my strong foundation in software development combined with my specialization in web technologies, I am confident in my ability to deliver high-quality and visually appealing websites that meet the unique needs of my clients.
